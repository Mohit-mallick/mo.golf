WELCOME TO PANCHKULA GOLF CLUB !

Panchkula Golf Club is an 18-hole golf course located in Panchkula, Haryana, India. Established in 1977, it is one of the oldest golf clubs in India. The course is designed by renowned golf course architect, Clive Clark, and features a mix of challenging and picturesque holes. Golfers of all Skill levels can enjoy the course, which has hosted several national and international tournaments.

ABOUT US

Located alongside the majestic River Ghaggar and set against the backdrop of the Shivalik Hills, Panchkula Golf Club offers a harmonious blend of natural beauty and world-class amenities. Our facilities include an 18-hole championship golf course designed to challenge both professionals and weekend golfers while providing a serene environment for relaxation.In addition to golf, our modern clubhouse features a health club, gymnasium, well-stocked bar, relaxing lounge, and a multi-cuisine restaurant. Our versatile party hall and terrace boast spectacular views, ideal for hosting unforgettable events and celebrations.
